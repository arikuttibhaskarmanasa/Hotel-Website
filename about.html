ABOUT OUR HOTELS

With a remarkable legacy spanning 50 years, Pai Hotels has established itself as a renowned name in the hospitality industry. Our family-run business has become synonymous with top-notch facilities, exceptional services, and unparalleled comfort, making us a trusted choice for discerning travelers. Operating in major cities of Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh such as Bangalore, Mysore, Hubli, and Tirupati, each Pai Hotel offers a delightful stay experience that combines luxury and warm hospitality.

Step into any of our 3-star hotels and immerse yourself in an ambiance of refined elegance. Our properties are meticulously designed with eye-catching decor that creates a captivating atmosphere. Whether you are visiting for business or leisure, our hotels provide a welcoming environment that caters to the diverse needs of our guests.

Indulge in the mouth-watering culinary offerings at our restaurants, where expert chefs craft exquisite dishes to satisfy every palate. Whether you are hosting a special event or looking for a comfortable venue for social gatherings, our magnificent banquet halls provide the perfect setting. Experience the comfort and convenience that have made Pai Hotels the preferred choice for luxurious stays.
